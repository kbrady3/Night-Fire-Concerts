Volunteer

Volunteer for Night Fire!



Night Fire Concerts relies completely on volunteers. Can you help make this Praise and Worship concert a great evening for everyone?

Volunteers are needed to help with:
Parking
Seating
Greeting /Information
Food Truck Crowd Management
Hospitality Tent Staffing
Donation Monitoring
Waste Management
Stage Set-up/Tear Down

If you volunteer you will get a free Night Fire T-shirt, get guaranteed Amphitheater seating of your choice, and help others have a great evening of praise and worship.

You will receive a confirmation email and reminders from Sign-Up. Your volunteer assignment and shift will be confirmed via email.

On the day of the concert, at the Volunteer Tent, please check-in to learn your volunteer station, get your free T-Shirt and receive any last minute info youâ€™ll need.
